Frequently Asked Questions

Manage FAQs for users and providers

How do I become a service provider?

To become a service provider, you need to:

  1. Register an account on our platform
  2. Complete your profile with all required information
  3. Submit necessary documents for verification
  4. Wait for our team to verify your account
  5. Once approved, you can start offering services

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex)
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfer
  • Digital Wallets (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • UPI payments

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the scheduled service time
  • 50% charge for cancellations within 24 hours of the service
  • Full charge for no-shows or cancellations less than 2 hours before service
  • Emergency cancellations due to valid reasons may be waived with documentation

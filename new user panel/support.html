Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Booking a service is simple and takes just a few minutes:

  1. Browse our services and select the one you need
  2. Click the "Book Now" button
  3. Choose your preferred date and time
  4. Fill in your address and any special instructions
  5. Proceed to payment

You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details once your booking is complete.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Google Pay
  • Apple Pay
  • Bank Transfer

All payments are securely processed through our encrypted payment gateway.

How can I reschedule or cancel my booking?

You can manage your bookings through the "My Bookings" section:

  1. Go to "My Bookings" in your account
  2. Find the booking you want to modify
  3. Click "Reschedule" or "Cancel"
  4. Follow the prompts to complete the action

Note: Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the service may incur a fee. Rescheduling is free if done at least 12 hours before the service time.

How long does a service typically take?

Service duration varies depending on the type of service:

  • Standard cleaning: 2-4 hours
  • Plumbing repair: 1-3 hours
  • Electrical work: 2-5 hours
  • Appliance installation: 1-4 hours

Your service professional will provide a more accurate time estimate based on your specific requirements during the booking process.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Your safety is our top priority. We have implemented several measures:

  • All professionals undergo thorough background checks
  • Real-time tracking of service professionals
  • Contactless payment options
  • COVID-19 safety protocols including masks and sanitization
  • 24/7 customer support for any concerns

You can also rate your service professional after each service to help us maintain quality standards.
